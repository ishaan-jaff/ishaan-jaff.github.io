Experience: Tedx Youth Talk [2016]

During the Summer of 2016 I was a Tedx Speaker at Neerja Modi School, Jaipur, India. I used this opportunity to talk about the lessons I learned from competing in the Indian Swimming Nationals for 10 years. The Talk is about my experiences with failures in sport, how I dealt with those failures and how these lessons can be applied to our everyday lives. I hope/hoped to encourage more Indian children to pursue sports at a high level.
