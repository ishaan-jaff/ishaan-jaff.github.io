Projects: RaffleChain

Winner, Best Implementation: Silicon Valley Developer Hackathon

When was the last time you entered a Raffle and were sure the outcome was truly randomized? Or that the outcome was truly unbiased?

RaffleChain(Raffle Built on Blockchain) aims to solve this problem. RaffleChain is a decentralized Web Application that uses the Nebulas Blockchain Technology to host completely randomized and decentralized Raffles.

Once we had completed our project, one of the Hackathon Sponsors: F50 offered to sponsor our project by offering to let us use two tickets to an F50 conference during our Project Demo (Our demo involved hosting a Raffle for these tickets at the hackathon).

Our Team also ended up winning "Best Implementation" at the Hackathon
